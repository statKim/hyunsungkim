About

I am a Ph.D. student from Department of Statistics at Seoul National University. Prior to that, I received my M.A. in Statistics and B.A. in Applied Statistics at Chung-Ang University under the supervision of Prof. Yaeji Lim.

My research interests include Functional Data Analysis, Non-Euclidean Data Analysis and Statistical Dimension Reduction. Currently, my main research work includes developing functional clustering and quantile based dimension reduction method for Riemannian functional data.

I love R coding and making documents using Rmarkdown. I sometimes write on my personal blog about Statistics, R, etc.
