About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Statistics at Seoul National University, under the supervision of Prof. Junyong Park. I am also a member of High Dimensional Multiple Testing Lab. Prior to that, I received my M.A. in Statistics and B.A. in Applied Statistics at Chung-Ang University under the supervision of Prof. Yaeji Lim.

My research interests include Functional Data Analysis, High-Dimensional Data Analysis, and Multiple Testing.

I love R coding and making documents using Rmarkdown. I sometimes write on my personal blog about Statistics, R, etc.
